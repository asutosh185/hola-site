2018 will be a year of technology outbreak. This will the change the way how the marketeers focus on brand marketing. With this paradigm shift, the emphasis on mobile users will increase. The simple well researched reason – By the end of 2018 nearly 50% of people will have smart phones.

With such an explosive growth, 74% of the marketeers expect mobile budgets to grow big in 2018. Read more on why mobile marketing is important.

Here are some of the upcoming trends to act on in 2018.

Smart Content: Content will rule the marketing discussion in 2018 as well. Smart content means personalized content which changes depending on who is looking at it. According to HubSpot, calls to action using their smart content feature performed 42% better than generic calls to action. 

This encourages relationship driven marketing and generates better output from your content.

Mobile first indexing: Google announced a year back that it will rank the sites based on their mobile version and not the desktop version.

At the SMX West conference, Google’s Gary Illyes said that they will launch the mobile-first index when results are “quality neutral.”

As the mobile traffic is increasing day by day, it is imperative to prioritize mobile content. 



With such spike, more marketeers are targeting mobile users for their brand marketing. Use hola cards to reach your prospects on any smart device. Hola cards enable you to create animated marketing message for your brand and engage with your audience using call to action. Try it out here.

Progressive web apps: It is an emerging technology and it will be the next big thing. It allows everyone to access the basic content of the webpage irrespective of the user’s browser or internet connection. With better internet and browser software, the advanced quality web page will be displayed. This alternative to a mobile app, can help your organization reach wider market.

Use of Artificial Intelligence: 80% of all marketing executives predict AI will transform marketing by 2020. It can help marketeers improve customer interaction. The data gathered over time is collated by AI and used to make intelligent assumptions to increase your leads. It can also help to create brand specific content that appeals to customers. Interested to know more? Read here.

Micromoments: Micromoments are the game changer. It is the short and precise time when brands can interact with the consumer. This buzz has led Marketeers rewrite the rule of marketing. You have to address the need of the consumer at that particular moment. Marketeers have to head towards an era of assistance to cater to the immediate need of consumers.

While the landscape of mobile marketing is changing all the time, it also presents more opportunities than ever to brands that use the described trends and technologies to stand out from the competition and capture the attention of their prospects and customers.
