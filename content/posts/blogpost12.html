AI Application in Digital Marketing: 5 ways how it makes your life easy

AI refers to the method of creating intelligent machines that are programmed to behave rationally and take informed decisions like a human would do. AI enabled machine can learn, reason and correct itself based on the past data or its experience.

Many aspects of AI are used in businesses these days. Digital marketing is also not left behind.

Below are the 5 ways AI help you transform your company’s digital marketing:

Content: Simple content can be developed automatically using AI. Content like stock updates, sports matches, quarterly earnings reports and other niche areas can be generated. It can also curate the existing content. It can show the relevant content from past behavior to the visitors of your site and personalize it as well. For example, “Customer’s who bought X also bought Y” section on the websites.

Chatbots: Due to increased competition, customer support is of vital importance to businesses. Chatbots, fueled by AI, are designed to interpret and answer customer queries in real time. It not only attracts the potential customers but also help in customer retention.

Predictive Analytics: It is a method of predicting future events. It uses many techniques to predict customer behavior. For example, predicting conversion rate, customer retention and commercials. This insight can also help you to create more impactful marketing campaigns.

Ad Targeting: Achieve more effective ad placement by assessing the vast amount of historical data by AI. It helps in determining which ads to target to specific customers, and at what stage in the buying cycle. It also serves the most effective content at the right time to the right set of customers.

Marketing automation: It helps marketers automate and measure repetitive tasks like regular emails and social media updates to increase efficiency and reduce human errors. AI allows you to understand your customers. It segments your customers based on several metrics like purchasing habits, behavioral traits, region of activity and generates buyer personas. Thus you can customize your offerings that resonates well with the target segment and approach them at the right platform. Hola Designer gives you the ability to set reminders and add automation to multiple campaigns.
