As this is know that technology has evolved from enormous size first generation computers to an era of smartphones that fit our palms. With the revolution in technology, the possibilities of mobile application has become limitless.

Similarly, advertisements have seen a trend shift. Starting from newspaper and magazine based advertisements, it has grown rapidly to wider platforms. Radio, TV, internet and now the mobile devices.

Let’s see the top most reason why Marketing on smart devices has become so much important:

Mobile reaches more and more people: Of the 6.8 Billion people on the planet, 4 Billion of them use a mobile phone. Only 3.5 Billion use a toothbrush.

It is an alarming rate with which the mobile usage is expanding. It has now become an accessory that people carry with them every day and at all times. This shows how easily Marketers can reach wider range of potential customers 24/7.

Time spent on mobile is growing: People spend more that 4 hours daily on their phone. The time spent on the phone is mix of time spent on social media, e-commerce, searching and browsing. This provides a clear opportunity to capture the attention of consumers. Advertising trend is also showing the clear path. It is estimated that by 2019, nearly 72% of marketing dollars will be spent developing apps and advertisements for mobile platforms.

Mobile commerce : By 2020, mobile commerce will account for 45 percent of all e-commerce activities — compared to 20.6 percent in 2016.

With the help of mobile specific advertising, search engine marketing and mobile friendly web pages, marketers can tap the mobile revenue stream and get in front of shoppers at the right time.

Mobile Ads : Mobile devices influenced sales to the tune of over $1.4 trillion in 2016 and is projected to reach about $2.05 trillion by the year 2020.

As you can see, it is an attractive option. Business can leverage this platform for enhancing opportunities and driving performance. It can address the target audience at the time of need. It is more personalized form of marketing that brings you closer to the customer psychology.

SMS Marketing : According to a study, an astounding 98% of people read their sms within 3 minutes. This is a great promotional tool and has quick customer engagement. Using SMS marketers can reach wider demographics of people within a short span of time. However with its 160 character limitation SMS severely under utilizes the capabilities of a smart phone.
