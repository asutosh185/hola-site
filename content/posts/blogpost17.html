The time has come to move to a realm of interactive video and leave static video behind. While it is true that static video is still an effective content strategy as 43% of people want to see more video content from marketers (source: https://www.hubspot.com/marketing-statistics). But the customer expectations are changing. Interactive and personalized experience demand is on the rise. Interactive Videos meet this demand and help businesses to enhance customer relationships.

Higher Engagement

Interactive videos give control to the users. When users feel the control, they stay more engaged. Another trick is content tailored and customized to user needs. It can capture attention, motivate action and increase conversion ratio.

Companies that foster positive customer engagement always have the edge over competitors. While watching a video, more than 50% of the experience is based on emotion. Emotion drive decisions. Interactive videos help you to create emotionally engaged customers. These customers are 3X more likely to recommend the company’s brand and 3X more likely to repurchase the company’s product or service. 

Source: Forbes Insights (https://www.forbes.com/forbesinsights/pitney_bowes_ces/index.html)

Customer Service

Interactive videos can enhance overall customer experience and improve customer engagement and build trust. 

According to a study by the Customer Contact Council, a division of the Corporate Executive Board (source: https://hbr.org/2010/07/stop-trying-to-delight-your-customers), Two critical findings emerged that should affect every company’s customer service strategy. First, delighting customers doesn’t build loyalty; reducing their effort—the work they must do to get their problem solved. Second, acting deliberately on this insight can help improve customer service, reduce customer service costs, and decrease customer churn.

Interactive Videos can be used for self-service. It reduces the time and effort required by our customers to get in contact with company’s support staff to solve a problem. 

IV allows you to add features like call to action, download information, documentation that makes the video self -sufficient and informative for the customer.

Entertain Customers

The most entertaining videos win customer heart and their attention. Don’t shy away from being creative and using the full power of IV in providing the personalized user experience.

Businesses understand the criticality of using IV in their content strategy. It can help not only to build good relation with the customer but also improve your leads and conversion ratio.
