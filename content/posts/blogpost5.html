According to Business Wire, the global interactive Media Marketing market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 10.68% over the period 2014-2019. Below are the ways which interactive media can deliver a great marketing campaign.

1.Market Presence - it helps grow your market presence and campaign effectiveness through emphasized brand recognition. When you provide your customer with an experience that is interactive and dynamic, it helps them overcome the challenges they face in understanding your product and services. They will remember their experience with you and this helps in brand recall.

2.Engage Customers - Interactive media such as animation is engaging, shareable and synergystic. It is helping marketers to reach and engage customers in much more personal and impactful ways, adding depth to user experience.

3.Analyze Performance - A huge benefit to utilizing interactive media in your marketing campaigns based on meaningful interactions, your customers are engaging with your current campaigns. You can learn a great deal about them and what emotions are driving their purchasing decisions.

4. Generate ROI - Investing in quality interactive media such as Hola Cards, interactive animated messages ultimately help you boost your Product/service promotions, increase engagement and ultimately boost conversions and ROI. Also, it differentiates you from your competition.



5. Visuals are processed faster- Visuals such as moving image, Animation & videos are processed 60,000 X faster in the brain than plain text. Based on this fact, it's easy to understand why interactive content precedence in grabbing a person's undivided attention and interest.

6. Drive Traffic - Interactive advertisements can help drive traffic on your website and social media channels, boost engagement and generate more views. Thus it is an investment worth making.

6. Gain Followers - Interactive media generates emotions,encourages users to instantly share your campaign, eventually increasing your followers and reach wider audiences. Hence, there is a possible chance that interactive media can help to provide information in a much clearer and easier to follow manner. Besides being easy to use they are shareable over various channels like email, SMS, whatsapp and other social media.
