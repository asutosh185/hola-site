With the revolution of new technologies, the marketing trends are also changing. Being a marketer, it definitely deserves your time and attention to find out the upcoming opportunities in this field and prepare your team for the challenges.

Big Data: Big data is a term for extremely large and complex chunks of data. It is expanding on 3 fronts at an increasing rate – Data velocity, Data Volume and Data variety. This huge data can be analysed to reveal meaningful customer insights and do predictive analysis. It can be exploited to judge how best to approach the potential customers. The marketers can use the data to increase the sales through personalization on the websites and email marketing.

Marketing Automation:A lot of repetitive marketing tasks such as email, social media and website actions can be automated using marketing automation tools. It helps you reach the right people with the right content at the right time. It allows marketers to spend more time on the important strategic tasks. Some of the good marketing automation tools are Hubspot, Pardot, Hola Designer, Marketo and ActiveCampaign.

Mobile Marketing: It is a multi-channel online marketing technique designed to delivery to mobiles and various hand held devices. As per Mobile Marketing Association SMoX Study , just by optimizing your content for mobile, marketing would be 2.6% more effective at driving sales. It is projected that by the end of 2017, 60% of e-commerce visits will start on a mobile device. Mobile Marketing can be used to drive business growth effectively. Mobile marketing can be done using App based marketing, ingame mobile marketing, location based marketing, Mobile search ads and sms marketing.



Source: Comscore

Social Media Marketing:It is a process of gaining customers through social media sites. Marketers can use social media platform to promote their products or services. It can be used to increase brand engagement and win new customers. 71% of consumers who have had a good social media service experience with a brand are likely to recommend it to others.



Source: ServerCake

Paid Search Marketing: It is a paid marketing technique where the advertisement is displayed under the sponsored listing of the search engine. You pay each time your ad is clicked (Pay-Per-Click) or when your ad is displayed (Cost per impression). Google Adwords is the most popular paid search marketing platform, followed by bing Ads and Yahoo Search Ads.

Affiliate Marketing: It is a pay per performance marketing method. It is a commission based method where the business pays to its affiliate only when they get a lead or a sale. Its success totally depends on finding the right affiliate. The Digital Marketing must develop right affiliate marketing strategy for better results.

Wearable Mobile Devices: By 2018 almost 350 million wearable devices will be in use worldwide. (source: CCS Insight) If you decide to use wearable technology for your brand, you have to focus on more visual driven elements than the text ones. Content must be mostly video or audio and fit the small size screen. People will use app more than the search engine. This means the traffic will move to app rather than search engines. Apps can also be better optimized for the small screen size of the wearable device rather than the web browser.
