Importance of digital marketing is crucial, irrespective of the industry. Today companies are building brand, increasing leads, improving customer engagement and boosting sales by innovative engagement activities online.

Following is an info graphic company respondents saying these will be very important for their digital marketing over the next few years



  1. Social media marketing: Enhance your reach exponentially and publisize your product information using social media. Social media platform can be used to share knowledge content with the audience in several forms. Interact with your users with the help of videos, blogs, online contests, etc.
  2. Explainer videos: Videos are everywhere and now account for 16% of all the content published by pharma companies on Facebook. Brands are competing more and more for attention. Videos provide the best way to get attention. Alongside, pharma companies can put lot of explainer videos about the correct use of product, faqs, dos and donts, user reviews etc.


  3. Influencer marketing: A survey showed that 70% of the sales are a result of an influence, which is mostly personal. With influencer marketing, the companies can subtly, seed the benefits of their product in the audience mind. It connects like-minded people to your message. Since it shows the personal experience of the influencer, it is a great way to build trust also.
  4. Anticipate consumer needs and reach them through digital marketing channels. In this era there is no one without a cell phone. Use the mobile technology to target patients and the physicians both to drive awareness and purchase reconsideration.
  5. Feedback for RnD: With the use of digital marketing, Pharma companies can get real-time feedback from consumers. This is very important for their research and development centers. The department can use the data to optimize their expenses and improve the product. Encouraging the seamless information flow between you and your customers will help predict the demand and supply better.

Digital platform plays a significant role to broaden your reach and opens omnichannel communication directly with your end customer. Stick to it and keep it in your high priority list, while you reap its benefits.
