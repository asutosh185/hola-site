Many marketers still struggle with the right social media strategy that can help build a better and more engaging presence.

Below are 5 secrets to a better and more engaging social media presence.

1.Concentrate on posting daily updates but do strike a balance - If you post irregularly, your audience will lose touch with your brand and will probably forget that you exist. On the other hand, if you post too frequently, your audience might lose interest in your updates. You could notice a negative effect on your engagement trends.

Always try to strike the right balance. At the same time ensure that your posts and updates have a good chance to be seen by your desired target audience.

According to Leo Widrich, it is advisable to post around 5-10 times a day on Twitter and 1-4 times a day on Facebook for optimal outcome.



2.Make the best use of data from social media channels - As marketers, you should know how to make the best use of data gathered from social channels e.g., Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Google+ and blogs.

Social media offers us a valuable opportunity to gather valuable marketing data but you must have clear goals what you’re looking for and what you’ll do with the information once you find it.

3.Engage in real time interactions -A great way to capture audience’s attention lies in real time interactions.

Daily real time interactions with users can not just help survive social media engagement but it will also help establish close and better connections and add more value to their user experience.

Indulge in more meaningful conversations with your audience to nurture a more engaging social media presence.

4.Post your content with relevant and innovative hashtags - Hashtags do matter a lot for today’s brands and every brand must invest in an effective hashtag strategy.

Be it any promotional campaign or special offers or daily updates, don’t forget to use hashtags as it makes it easy to track a promotion’s activity across many social platforms. It is like a label that makes it easier to find specific content and help members to filter information.

It also allows you to connect with new or existing audiences and begin a social conversation.
