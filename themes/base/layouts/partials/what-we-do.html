What we do

Hola Enterprise is a micro-ad platform for marketing and lead generation, through light weight & interactive animations

Create

Create your animated communication in minutes, using our pre-configured templates. Hola cards can play on any smart device.

Reach

Reach your audience over various channels like email, SMS, whatsapp and other social media platforms. No software installation needed to play the animations

Engage

Call To Actions allow your receivers to click and interact with the Hola Cards. Make their experience lively and engaging.

Analyse

Benefit from powerful reporting & analytics that help you track and understand user engagement. Get better ROI on marketing spends.
